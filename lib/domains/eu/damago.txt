damago

additional info:
- damago organizes government funded "retrainings" (German: Umschulung) for people who need to change careers. The retrainings are a shorter version (2 years) of the classic vocational training (3 years) young people do after highschool.
- the domain for the student e-mailaccounts is damago.eu
- the domain for the staff e-mailaccounts and the official website is damago.de
- daily attendence on premise is required by the Jobcenters/LabourAgencies.
- online participation is possible, but only if approved by the Berlin Jobcenters/LabourAgencies, e.g. for medical conditions.
